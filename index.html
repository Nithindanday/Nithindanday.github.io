The Alien Slug
Bananaslug
Slimy, slow
Eating, digesting, decomposing
The alien slug
Monochrome





Water-life
Waterstrider
Fast, jumpy
Leaping, gliding, striding
Macro-invertebrate, insect, crab, sea-life
Crawling, rolling, wandering
Tiny, slow
Hermit crab




                                          
WebSter
 Spiders
Global, creative
Creepy, crawling, hiding
The ever web-spinning creature
Aarchnid
                                     

The Cloudy Ocean

 

Like a pool of water open to all of nature

Like the power of the sun

Like raindrops dancing around the window on a stormy day  

Like a cloud crashing into mountains

Like a paradise for all of wildlife

Like a bright light reflecting on water

Like a planet of nature







WESTMINSTER WOODS

A shady forest

The rhythmic sound of the drilling woodpecker

The pleasant cool, foggy air

The damp air

The rough bark



Ignorance

Darkness

Cold, evil, unknown

Darkness



Tree house Forest

The leaf covered sky above

The peaceful silence

The unknown nature

The damp soil below me

The steps on the hillside

Cargo Net

The crystal clear stream below me

Peers screaming

The nothingness of fun

The cool breeze carrying the smell of redwood forests

The rough ropes poking and bruising
